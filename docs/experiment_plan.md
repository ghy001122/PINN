1. G1/E1: complete data-free GeoPhase PINN, declared sparse-anchor hybrid PINN, and pure-surrogate comparisons.
2. G2/E2: transition-localized spectral experts and phase/Joule homotopy with matched budgets, multiple seeds, and single-module ablations.

Each stage needs a new active-phase authorization and its own preregistered gate card.
