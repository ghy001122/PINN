This assessment is limited to synthetic numerical digital-twin benchmark evidence.

A third-zone SCI computational/method application paper is plausible if the manuscript stays narrow. The evidence chain now covers:

- frozen synthetic Ground Truth v1.1;
- sparse-port full-field ill-posedness;
- reduced `gamma_sub` target selection;
- confounding and prior-width audits;
- calibration tolerance and ODE spot-check;

- phase-transition stiffness residual preflight;
- phase-field inverse alignment smoke benchmark.

## Second-Zone Edge Gap

The project is still weaker for a second-zone edge submission because it lacks:

- measured device data;
- provenance-backed external curve digitization and fitting;
- a validated F-SPS superiority result;
- full 2D inverse training evidence;
- independent observability beyond terminal electrical ports.

## Recommended Position

Target the manuscript as a careful numerical inverse-identification benchmark. Do not sell it as experimental validation, complete device modeling, or sparse-port full-field recovery.
