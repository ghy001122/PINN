# Limitations V1

All claims are restricted to a synthetic numerical digital-twin benchmark.

1. No measured experimental data are used.
2. No full three-dimensional device simulation is claimed.
3. Sparse electrical port data do not uniquely recover full hidden fields.
4. `gamma_sub` is conditionally identifiable only under fixed or tightly bounded switching, conductivity, defect, and geometry priors.
5. `T_sw` is the dominant confounder and must be independently calibrated or tightly bounded.
6. External literature curve fitting remains blocked until provenance-backed digitized CSV/Sheet data are added.
7. F-SPS-PINN evidence remains appendix or future-work material and does not support superiority claims.
8. Sequential protocol results are synthetic simulator-backed preflight evidence, not experimentally validated protocol prescriptions.
