# Limitations Locked V1

1. Synthetic numerical digital-twin benchmark only.
2. No measured experimental validation.
3. No full 3D device model.
4. No sparse-port unique full hidden-field recovery.
5. `gamma_sub` recovery requires fixed or tightly bounded `T_sw`, `tau_m`, `sigma_on0`, `eta_A`, and micro-defect priors.
6. The 0.1 K tolerance is a synthetic audit threshold, not a real-device calibration requirement.
7. External curve fitting remains blocked until provenance-backed digitized numerical curves are added.
8. The quasi-2D preflight does not solve 2D inverse diagnosis.
9. F-SPS-PINN evidence remains appendix/future-work material.
