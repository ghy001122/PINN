## Why This Phase Is Active

The completed audits show:

- port-only full hidden-field recovery remains ill-posed for `delta_T`, `c_v`, `m`, and `sigma`;
- constrained `gamma_sub` inversion is the strongest manuscript mainline when switching and conductivity priors are fixed or tightly bounded;
- `T_sw` remains the limiting confounder and must be controlled in the claim boundary;
- dense response-surface, recoverability phase-diagram, protocol validation, weighted-objective, and statistical robustness audits add reviewer-facing breadth without changing the frozen benchmark;
- F-SPS-PINN v2 evidence remains method-development support only. The medium-budget subset is useful as appendix/future-work evidence and does not prove F-SPS superiority.
