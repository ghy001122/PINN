## Why This Phase Is Active

The completed high-throughput pack broadened the evidence base but left three reviewer-facing risks:

- the 2501 dense profile points are response-surface interpolation from 77 simulator-backed source points, not 2501 fresh ODE solves;
- single-protocol recommendations need a stronger value-of-information framing before being written as protocol-design evidence;
- the earlier F-SPS medium-budget subset executed only 8 cases and was coverage-imbalanced.

This phase addresses those risks while keeping the main manuscript claim narrow: synthetic numerical digital-twin sparse-port identifiability boundary plus conditional `gamma_sub` reduced inversion under fixed or tightly bounded priors.
