# Limitations V1

1. All results are synthetic numerical digital-twin evidence; no measured experimental validation has been completed.
2. The main model is one-dimensional and reduced-order. It is not full FEM/3D/device-grade multiphysics.
3. Sparse terminal data do not support unique arbitrary complete hidden-field recovery in the configured benchmark; the audit is not a universal impossibility proof.
4. \(\gamma_{\mathrm{sub}}\) is a lumped effective parameter, not an unconditional material constant.
5. Recovery is conditional on fixed microphysics and fixed or tightly bounded nuisance priors, especially \(T_{\mathrm{sw}}\).
6. The approximately `0.1 K` \(T_{\mathrm{sw}}\) marker is a synthetic threshold under the configured error criterion, not a real-device specification.
7. Noise, seed, observation-count, and off-grid robustness is conditional; wide \(T_{\mathrm{sw}}\) mismatch remains a reproducible failure region.
8. Protocol search is finite and benchmark-specific. The locked 720-case Figure 5 result is not experimental or globally optimal, and the broader 2400-case stress audit is supplementary negative evidence.
9. Response-surface sweeps reuse declared simulator anchors; dense surface points are not independent fresh ODE solves.
10. Public VO2 provenance and source semantics are now locked, but D0a fails its time-step convergence gate. No repository calibration or 13 V cross-voltage evaluation was run; this is not external or experimental validation.
11. V10 P1 is field-anchored and fails its strict interface gate; P2 is local linearized recovery and proceeds with rank-deficient selected protocols.
12. Segmented-terminal rank gain applies only to a three-parameter conductivity-profile basis and does not establish full 2D field recovery.
13. OASIS generalization is a ridge preflight, not trained neural-operator evidence; cross-material transfer fails strongly.
14. Full STL/Seiler reproduction, universal Fourier/F-SPS superiority, terminal-only 2D inverse solved, and experimental validation remain `forbidden`.
15. The versioned complete 1D PINN implementation passes its contract/manufactured audit but fails the fixed single-seed forward MVE. Reliability, sensitivity fidelity and inverse recovery remain `forbidden`.
16. The protocol-conditioned quotient hypothesis was not tested because D0a and N0 failed upstream gates. No negative or positive scientific conclusion about protocol-dependent quotient geometry follows from these stops.
