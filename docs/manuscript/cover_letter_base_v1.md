Please consider our manuscript for publication as a research article in

Using a frozen one-dimensional synthetic numerical benchmark, we show that
terminal conductance strongly constrains an integrated conductivity response

calibrated or tightly bounded. A factorial audit further separates calibration
from protocol effects and finds calibration to be the dominant contribution.
